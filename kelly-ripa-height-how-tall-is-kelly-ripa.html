    Kelly Ripa is a famous American actress and talk show host Since 2001, she has been the co-host of the syndicated morning talk show Live! with Kelly and Mark in various formats.

    She received her first significant break in 1990 when she was given the role of Hayley Vaughan on the well-known soap series “All My Children.”

    This part would end up being crucial in helping her develop professionally and establish herself as a gifted actress. Ripa garnered a sizable fan base and received considerable praise for her portrayal of the complicated and combative character Hayley.

    Kelly Ripa’s dynamic and vivacious personality started to emerge during her time on “All My Children,” inspiring her to look into alternatives outside of acting.

    She joined the renowned Regis Philbin as a co-host of “Live! with Regis and Kelly” in 2001.

    She easily changed from acting to being a television personality, opening a new chapter in her career.
