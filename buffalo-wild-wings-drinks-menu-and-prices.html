     Buffalo Wild Wings

     News: Buffalo Wild Wings, a well-loved restaurant and sports bar celebrated for its delectable wings and lively ambiance, was established in Columbus, Ohio, by Jim Disbrow, Scott Lowery, Bernard Spencer, and Elizabeth Brock. Since its inception, the brand has flourished, extending its reach to over 1200 locations across the globe.

    Buffalo Wild Wings presents an extensive array of beverages designed to satisfy every palate. Whether you’re seeking non-alcoholic options or indulging in boozy delights, the menu caters to a diverse range of preferences, ensuring there’s something for everyone.

    Non-Alcoholic Beverages

    Among the non-alcoholic options at Buffalo Wild Wings are refreshing drinks like Izze sparkling juice, Red Bull, Pepsi, Diet Pepsi, Mountain Dew, and more. Priced at an average of $20, these beverages offer an affordable and diverse selection.

    Alcoholic Beverages

    Buffalo Wild Wings caters to those in the mood for alcoholic beverages with an extensive selection. They feature popular beer brands such as Bud Light, Budweiser, Coors Light, Heineken, alongside craft beer options like Angry Orchard Crisp Apple Cider, Blue Moon Belgian White, Creature Comforts Tropicalia, and more. Additionally, they provide packs of beer cans for takeout orders.

    It’s important to note that all alcoholic beverages are exclusively available to patrons who are 21 years of age or older. The availability and prices may vary depending on the location of the Buffalo Wild Wings restaurant.
