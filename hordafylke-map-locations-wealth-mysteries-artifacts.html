    Table of contents

    Hordafylke Wealth, Mysteries, and Artifacts locations map

    There are 1 Ability Wealth location, 7 Mysteries, and 2 Artifacts in Hordafylke.

    Hordafylke Wealth locations map

    There is only one Wealth location in Hordafylke that is an Ability. We’ve broken them up into regions based (roughly) on the closest settlement or named region.

    By this point in the game, you can purchase ingots from most shops, so we’re going to stop walking you through collecting them. Once you build the Cartographer’s shop in Ravensthorpe, you can buy maps to all ingots as well. And since Cargo is always in Raid locations, we’ll let you collect those on your own as well.

    Alkrekstad

    1 - Ability: Mark of Death

    In Alrekstad, look for a house near the coast with a pig pen outside. Use Odin’s Sight to highlight a key in the pen. Grab the key and go to the shed on the property. Shoot the pulley suspending the net and drop down into the tunnel. Use the key to open the door to collect the Mark of Death Book of Knowledge.

    Hordafylke Mysteries locations map

    There are 7 Mystery locations in Hordafylke, including things like World Events, Cairns, Fly Agaric Hallucination Challenges, and a Legendary Animal. We’ve broken them up into regions based (roughly) on the closest settlement or named region.

    Helskip

    1 - Legendary Animal: Bear of the Blue Waters

    On an island in northern Hordafylke, you’ll meet the massive Bear of the Blue Waters, a hulking polar bear with a sword lodged in their shoulder. Aim for the weak spots first and be careful not to get too close. The Bear of the Blue Waters is huge and has a longer reach than other legendary animals. Dodge when they lunge and swipe at their back and side. Eventually you’ll wear them down, completing the Bear of Blue Waters Legendary Animal fight.

    2 - Drengr Fight: Orwig Neverdeath

    In the mountains to the northwest of Ulritken Peak, you’ll find a Drengr named Orwig Neverdeath who uses only shields when he fights. Your best bet is to shoot out his weak spots, wait for him to charge, and dodge out of the way. While he’s recovering you can get in some swipes, and then back off. We also were able to knock him off the ledge. If you kill him this way, you’ll still get credit for winning the Orwig Neverdeath Lost Drengr fight.

    Hlutrholt

    3 - Animus Anomaly: Hlutrholt

    Grid View

    On the northeast end of the southwest island, look for the telltale glint of an Animus Anomaly. Once it starts, avoid the hazard to make it to an area where you need to redirect a projector. Use that to climb to the next section.

    Proceed until you reach the cylinder. Climb up and continue to another section where you need to redirect a beam. Direct it to the rightmost position first and ride the platform over. When you reach that side, use the other beam to position the unstable platform ahead so you can use it to climb up.

    Ride the platform back to the left, and redirect the beam so that the closer unstable platform is in its leftmost position. Proceed, avoiding the hazards as you go.

    On the final ramp, a spiky hazard will slide toward you, so climb on top of the platforms and wait for it to pass. As soon as it passes while you’re on the second platform, sprint to the finish to complete the Hlutrholt Animus Anomaly.

    Torghatten Rock

    4 - Cairn: Torghatten Rock

    On the west side of the mountain that dominates the southwestern island, you’ll find some stones for a cairn. You can complete this one easily — you don’t even need to use all the stones. Stack three horizontally to complete the Torghatten Rock Cairn.

    5 - Fly Agaric: Hordafylke

    On the east side of the southwest island, eat the Fly Agaric mushrooms to begin a fight with five Jotun giants. Defeat them to complete the Fly Agaric Hallucination Challenge for Hordafylke.

    Alrekstad

    6 - World Event: The Rekindling

    In Alrekstad, you’ll meet the Raid-Loving Wife, a woman standing outside her house who misses the spark that raiding provided for her and her husband’s sex life. Go inside the house when she asks and break all the wooden crates. Then throw a torch on the hay. Let it burn as she and her husband get turned on to complete “The Rekindling” World Event.

    7 - World Event: Battle Born

    Follow the road east out of Alrekstad until you come to three kids standing near a flagpole(?). Speak to Kaija and she’ll invite you on a raid. Follow the kids west to the fort, and take out the four dummies (you’ll have to do all the “fighting” for them). Speak to Kaija again to complete the “Battle Born” World Event.

    Hordafylke Artifacts locations map

    There are 2 Artifacts in Hordafylke, and both are Flying Papers. By the time you’ve reached Hordafylke, you’ll have completed enough Artifacts that we’ve no longer going to walk you through them all. The map above will guide you to the area, and your Odin’s Sight ability will help you pin down the exact location.
