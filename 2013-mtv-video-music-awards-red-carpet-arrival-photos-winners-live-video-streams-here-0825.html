    The 2013 MTV Video Music Awards will be coming at us LIVE tonight with a show jam packed full of our favorite celebrities and hip-happening performers. Lady Gaga‘s scheduled to open the show with a dramatic performance and we’ve also been promised amazeballs sets from other chart toppers, including Bruno Mars, Macklemore & Ryan Lewis, Miley Cyrus, Robin Thicke, Katy Perry, Kanye West, and Justin Timberlake (recipient of this year’s Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award). Sound good? We think so!

    The awards will be coming at us LIVE from Brooklyn, New York and they’re set to begin at 9 PM EST — with the red carpet arrival action to begin at 8 PM EST. MTV will also be streaming behind-the-scenes coverage during the red carpet as well as the main show, so don’t forget to check out that goodness as well. Check below for the streams or CLICK HERE to head on over to the official website to get your All Access pass.

    We’ll be updating this post throughout the night with the VMA winners in each category, and we’ll also make sure you have a fabulous photo gallery filled with pictures of all the hot celebs — all of whom have stepped out for a night of wonderful music celebration. What are you most looking forward to? Hit up the comments section below. The performances were all really great — CLICK HERE.
