    Here's a list of every location where you can find Yellow Bromeliad flowers (and all craftable items) in Disney Dreamlight Valley.

    First Published December 19, 2022, 11:30

    The mysterious world of Disney Dreamlight Valley is filled to the brim with a wide variety of flowers scattered across the different biomes. One such flower is the Yellow Bromeliad, a material or ingredient used in Crafting or sold to villagers to earn Star Coins in Disney Dreamlight Valley.

    Since the Yellow Bromeliad flower only grows at certain biomes, it isn't easy to come across this material in the game. That said, here's all you need to know about finding Yellow Bromeliad flowers in Disney Dreamlight Valley, including its location and all craftable items.

    Where To Find Yellow Bromeliad In Disney Dreamlight Valley

    Yellow Bromeliads can only be found in the Sunlit Plateau Biome, located left of the Village Plaza and above the Glade of Trust. However, the area is locked by default in Disney Dreamlight Valley. Therefore, to get Yellow Bromeliads, you'll first need to unlock Sunlit Plateau Biome by spending 7,000 Dreamlight to remove the Giant Night Thorns blocking the area's entrance.

    After unlocking it, roam around the Sunlit Plateau Biome to find Yellow Bromeliad in Disney Dreamlight Valley. Apart from this, here’s a list of all the new materials encountered in the Sunlit Plateau.

    • Vanilla
    • Dry Wood
    • Soya Seed (Gathered by Clearing Thorns)
    • Gold Ore
    • Tourmaline
    • Red Bromeliad
    • Pink Bromeliad
    • Yellow Bromeliad
    • Orange Houseleek
    • Pink Houseleek
    • Yellow Houseleek
    • Chili Pepper, Cotton, Zucchini, Soya (Goofy’s Stand)
    • Salmon, Tilapia (Blue Fishing Hotspot)
    • Walleye (Gold Fishing Hotspot)

    Yellow Bromeliad - Craftable Items

    Yellow Bromeliad is used as an ingredient to forging the following items in Disney Dreamlight Valley:

    • Impatiens and Bromeliad Pot
    • Pink, Yellow, and Blue Balloon Arch
    • Pink, Yellow, and Blue Balloon Cluster
    • Short Birdhouse
    • Pink Hydrangea (3)
    • Yellow and White Flower Rectangle

    Every flower also has a different price, and players can sell it at any of Goofy's Stalls. When it comes to Yellow Bromeliad, Wanderers can sell this flower to villagers and earn 73 Star Coins. Flowers respawn daily, so ensure you collect them to earn plenty of Star Coins. And that's everything you need to know about finding Yellow Bromeliad in Disney Dreamlight Valley.
