    How is Joe Keery's Dating Life Going with Maika Monroe?

    The 29 years old Joe Keery has been romantically linked with his long time girlfriend, Maika Monroe since 2017. She is an actress who starred in the commercial films, Independence Day: Resurgence, The Guest and It Follows.

    Talking about how their love romance began, the beautiful couple first met each other while shooting the film, After Everything, released in 2018. Finally, they made their relationship public in the summer of 2017 and is still going extremely strong.

    Since then Joe and Maika have been spotted together in various public places. In 2017, they attended the red carpet premiere of Stranger Things 2. Moreover, they even upload a romantic and cute photo of a boyfriend and girlfriend on their respective social Instagram account. 

    Rumored Girlfriend-Emily Schexnayder

    The 1.8 m tall No Activity star was said to be in a relationship with a girl, Emily Schexnayder. The media found them as a couple when he shared a screenshot of his phone’s screen on his Instagram in December 2016.

    Also, Emily had regularly posted their pictures on her social media account. However, the reported couple has not officially announced that they were dating. On the other, his current girlfriend, Maika, hooked up with many A list actors named Zac Efron, Taylor Lautner, Liam Hemsworth, and Boyd Holbrook.

    How Much is Joe Keery Net Worth?

    Joe Keery has an impressive net worth of $2 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He has made his fortune from his successful acting and music career. Further, his salary is expected to be around $50 Thousand per year.

    Keery is best known for his role breakout role as Steve Harrington on Netflix hit series, Stranger Things. Its cast members are assumed to making from $250,000 to $300,000 per episode. It stars Shannon Purser, Millie Bobby Brown, Maya Hawke, Cara Buono and others. 

    Not only this but he has done played a number of roles in TV series like Sirens, Chicago Fire, Empire, and No Activity. Similarly, his film Molly's Game earned $59 million worldwide in 2017. Aides from his acting career, he is a musician and is known as the guitarist/vocalist for the psych-rock band Post Animal.

    Their debut studio album named Perform the Most Curious Water Activities was released in December 2015. More to that, he has released several singles like I’m Not Smart and Slowbro.

    Bio and Early Life

    Joe Keery was born on 24 April 1992 in Newburyport, Massachusetts, the United States as Joseph David Keery. His parents are David Keery, an architect and Nina Keery, an English professor.

    Keery has four sisters named Caroline Keery, Lizzy Keery, Kate Keery, and Emma Keery. He attended the Newburyport High School and graduated from the DePaul University at  The Theatre School in 2014. Joe holds American citizenship and is of White ethnicity.
