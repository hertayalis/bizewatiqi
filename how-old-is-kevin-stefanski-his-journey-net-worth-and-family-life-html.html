    Stefanski’s NFL Journey

    In 2006, Kevin Stefanski entered the NFL as an assistant coach for the Minnesota Vikings. During his time with the Vikings, which lasted until 2019, he took on various roles. Notably, he became the offensive coordinator during his final two years there. But his journey didn’t stop in Minnesota. In 2020, he took on the responsibility of leading the Cleveland Browns. With a fresh team and a lot of determination, Stefanski guided the Browns to an impressive 11-5 record. This accomplishment earned him the prestigious NFL’s Coach of the Year award in 2020.
