    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Lanny Poffo was born in Calgary, Alberta, Canada, to a family deeply rooted in the wrestling industry.     Wrestling Career

    Poffo began his wrestling career in the 1970s, working in various regional promotions before joining the World Wrestling Federation (WWF, now WWE) in 1985. He wrestled under the ring names “Leaping” Lanny Poffo and “The Genius,” gaining popularity for his high-flying moves and poetic promos.

    Signature Moves and Promos

    As “Leaping” Lanny, Poffo was known for his acrobatic style, including his signature move, the moonsault. As “The Genius,” he delivered eloquent and often humorous promos, reciting self-written poems that added a unique touch to his character.
